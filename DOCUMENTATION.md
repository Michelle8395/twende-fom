# Twende Fom Documentation

## Overview
**Twende Fom** (Swahili for "Let's go for the event") is a social saving and group-planning platform designed for the modern generation. It empowers friend groups to pool resources, track contributions, and achieve shared goals, whether it’s a road trip to Mombasa, a new gaming console for the hangout, or a group birthday gift.

By combining financial transparency with an engaging, social-first interface, Twende Fom eliminates the friction and awkwardness of group money management.

---

## Why Twende Fom is Unique

### 1. Social Accountability & Transparency
Unlike traditional banking apps or split-expense tools (like Splitwise) which focus on *debt*, Twende Fom focuses on **active saving**. Every member can see the group's progress in real-time. This visibility creates a healthy social nudge, ensuring everyone stays committed to the goal.

### 2. Milestone-Driven Engagement
The platform doesn't just show numbers; it celebrates progress. Milestone alerts (e.g., "50% reached!") and automated activity feeds keep the momentum high and the group excited about the upcoming event.

### 3. Clean and user friendly UI/UX design that alligns with the theme.
The app features a custom-built "Vibrant Dark Theme." With glassmorphism components, neon glow effects, and animated progress rings, Twende Fom feels more like a social experience than a financial utility. It’s designed to be used and enjoyed, not just checked.

### 4. Fom ID Ecosystem
Every user is assigned a unique **Fom ID** (e.g., `FOM-8821`). This simplifies joining clubs and identifying friends, creating a sense of belonging to an exclusive community of planners and "doers."

## Why It’s Important (Value Proposition)

### For Users:
- **No More "Flaky" Friends:** By formalizing the saving process, the app filters out those who aren't serious about the plan before the booking stage.
- **Stress-Free Planning:** Centralizes the financial aspect of group events, so the focus remains on the fun.
- **Micro-Savings:** Members can contribute small amounts over time, making large goals (like a KES 100k trip) manageable for everyone.

### For Investors:
- **Niche Market Leader:** While fintech is crowded, "Social Saving for Gen Z/Millennials" in the East African context is a high-growth, underserved niche.
- **Viral Growth Loops:** The "Fom Club" model is inherently viral. To use the app, users must invite their friends, leading to low-cost organic user acquisition.
- **Trust & Data:** By capturing group saving habits, the platform builds a unique dataset on social spending, opening doors for future integrations with travel agencies, retailers, and event organizers.

---

## Key Features

- **Dashboard:** A bird’s-eye view of your total savings across all active Foms and personalized stats.
- **Fom Clubs:** Create private groups with specific target amounts and deadlines.
- **Real-Time Progress Rings:** High-fidelity visual indicators of how close the group is to its goal.
- **Activity Feed:** A chronological timeline of contributions and milestones for full transparency.
- **User Onboarding:** Seamless flow to create a profile or join an existing club using a Club ID.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React with TypeScript for a robust, type-safe user experience.
- **Styling:** Modern CSS utilizing variables for dynamic "Neon" effects and Glassmorphism.
- **Icons:** Lucide React for a clean, professional look.
- **Backend:** Node.js & Express (Scalable and fast).
- **Architecture:** Modular component-based structure (ProgressRing, ActivityItem, StatBox) for easy maintenance and expansion.
